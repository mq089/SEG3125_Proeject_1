Meet Our Team

John Wick

Michael Emerson

John Wick founded GeeGees Grooming in 2014. John, an alumni at the University of Ottawa discovered that during his schooling that he found it very difficult to take care of his newly adopted puppy. After graduating from University, John decided to open GeeGees Grooming in order to help other dog owners who are in a similar situation that he used to be in. And for his love of dogs of course! John specializes in individual walks & overnight homecare.

Michael Emerson is a research professor from the University of Ottawa in the Faculty of Science. Michael specializes in animal science and even taught John during his studies! Once he heard about GeeGees Grooming, he decided to join the team due to his love & fascination with animals. Being an older gentlemen, Michael isn't as spry as he used to be. Michael specializes in grooming.

Sofia Al-Azwar

Shamier Anderson

Sofia Al-Azwar is GeeGees Grooming's full-time specialist. Sofia grew up on a farm in Clarence-Rockland, Ontario. Her farm had many animals including several dogs which she raised and trained. After moving to Ottawa, Sofia decided to join GeeGees Grooming for the familiar work environment. Sofia specializes in all of our services.

Shamier Anderson is GeeGees Grooming's student specialist. Currently pursuing an undergraduate degree in enivronmental studies, he is a natural bookworm & explorer at heart! Shamier has his own dog who he loves going on hikes with. He is the newest addition to the GeeGees Grooming team and our first member to have come from Carleton University. Shamier specializes in outdoor services which include field trips along with individual & group walks.
