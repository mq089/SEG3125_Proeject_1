About us

At GeeGees Grooming we provide all-inclusive services for your canine companions! Our mission is to provide the best care and personalized services for your pets. As students & dog lovers, we understand that you may not have time to take care of your best friend. We understand the importance of maintaining your pet's health. Our team provides an array of services to ensure your pet stays happy and healthy.

Our contact hours are 7:00am - 5:00pm all week, we also provide 24 hour care. Please see appointment times for availability.
