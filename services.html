Our Services

Individual Walks [$30/hour]

We will take your dog on a 1-on-1 walk throughout the neighbourhood.

Group Walks [$25/hour]

We will take your dog on a group walk with other dogs throughout the neighbourhood.

Homecare [$50/hour]

We will look after your dog at our home while you're gone. This service includes group walks, food, and treats for your dog.

Grooming [$25 per session]

We will groom and freshen up your dog based on your preferences.

Field Trips [$35/hour]

We will take your dog in a group to a dog park while you're gone. This service includes a small gift and treats for your dog.
